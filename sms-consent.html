SMS Consent & Privacy

How we obtain consent for SMS communications and manage your preferences

How We Obtain SMS Consent

Common Sense Systems respects your privacy and follows all applicable regulations regarding SMS communications. We obtain consent to send you SMS messages through the following methods:

Written Consent

We may obtain your written consent through:

  • Online forms where you explicitly agree to receive SMS communications
  • Physical forms or contracts that include SMS consent language
  • Digital agreements that clearly state SMS messaging terms

Verbal Consent

We may obtain verbal consent during:

  • Phone conversations where you explicitly agree to receive SMS messages
  • In-person meetings where SMS communication preferences are discussed
  • Consultations where you request SMS updates or notifications

Opt-In Keywords

You may consent to SMS communications by:

  • Texting specific keywords to our phone number (425) 979-2282
  • Responding to initial messages with confirmation keywords
  • Using our automated systems that require explicit opt-in confirmation

Types of Messages You May Receive

Once you've provided consent, you may receive the following types of SMS messages:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Project updates and status notifications
  • Important service announcements
  • Response messages to your inquiries
  • Occasional promotional offers (with separate consent)

How to Revoke Your Consent

You can stop receiving SMS messages from us at any time using any of these methods:

Text STOP

Simply reply STOP to any SMS message you receive from (425) 979-2282. You will receive a confirmation message that you have been unsubscribed.

Important Information

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your service needs and preferences. We do not send mass marketing messages unless you have specifically opted in to promotional communications.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. Common Sense Systems does not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier may.

Supported Carriers

Our SMS service works with all major US carriers. If you experience delivery issues, please contact us directly.

Privacy Policy

Your phone number and SMS preferences are protected under our privacy policy. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
