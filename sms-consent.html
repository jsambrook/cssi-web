Questions About Text Messages?

"What if I'm not sure I said yes?"

Don't worry! We keep records of all opt-ins. If you're getting messages you don't want, just text "STOP" and we'll stop immediately.

"Can I sign up again if I change my mind?"

Absolutely! Just text "YES" to +1 (833) 570-5023 anytime.

"What if the messages aren't helpful?"

Text "HELP" to get our contact information, or call us at (425) 979-2282 (voice only). We want to make sure our messages are useful to you.

"Will you sell my phone number?"

No. We only use your phone number to send you the messages you've requested. We don't share or sell your contact information.
