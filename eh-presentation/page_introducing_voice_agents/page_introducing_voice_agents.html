Introducing Voice Agents

What Are Voice Agents?

Voice agents are conversational AI systems designed for natural voice interactions via phone or mobile apps. They understand speech, process requests, and respond in human-like conversation.

"Think of them as your most knowledgeable staff member who never sleeps"

Key Capabilities

📞

Handle Inquiries

Answer common questions about services, hours, insurance, and procedures with accurate, up-to-date information.

📅

Book Appointments

Schedule, reschedule, and confirm appointments across multiple providers and locations seamlessly.

ℹ️

Provide Information

Share directions, preparation instructions, and post-care guidance tailored to each patient's needs.

🌙

24/7 Availability

Operate around the clock, ensuring patients can access support when they need it most.

🛠️

Tool Use

Voice agents can use tools to interface with external systems.

👩‍💻️

Programmable

Voice agents can be programmed to perform a very wide range of tasks.
